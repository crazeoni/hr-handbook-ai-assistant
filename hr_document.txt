# Welcome to AcmeCorp!

We’re excited to have you on board. This handbook covers our most common HR policies and employee FAQs.

## Working Hours

Our standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Employees are expected to work 40 hours per week.

Flexible work schedules may be arranged with your manager’s approval.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid time off per year. PTO must be scheduled at least two weeks in advance and approved by your supervisor.

Sick leave is separate from PTO. Employees receive 10 sick days annually.

## Maternity and Paternity Leave

AcmeCorp provides 16 weeks of paid maternity leave and 4 weeks of paid paternity leave. Additional unpaid leave can be requested through HR.

## Remote Work Policy

Remote work is allowed on a hybrid basis. Employees may work remotely up to three days a week, depending on team needs.

All remote work must be logged via the internal HR portal.

## Health Insurance

All full-time employees are enrolled in our health insurance plan after 30 days of employment. The plan includes medical, dental, and vision coverage.

## Performance Reviews

Employees receive performance reviews twice a year (June and December). Feedback is based on individual goals, teamwork, and initiative.

## Expense Reimbursement

Employees can request reimbursement for business-related expenses such as travel, meals, or equipment purchases.

All claims must be submitted within 30 days using the company reimbursement form.

## IT Help and Equipment

For technical issues, contact the IT support team at it@acmecorp.com.

New employees receive a laptop, monitor, and access to all required software tools on day one.

## Code of Conduct

We expect all employees to behave professionally, treat each other with respect, and follow company policies.

Discrimination, harassment, and unprofessional conduct will not be tolerated.

For more information or to report an issue, contact HR at hr@acmecorp.com.
